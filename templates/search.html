Mixed Media is a simultaneous online news search for both conservative and liberal media.
It is especially effective in comparing breaking news reports from right and left media outlets.
It is a tool for raising awareness about everyday media bias.

Methodology
6 popular media outlets from the right and left have been selected based on bias ratings from mediabiasfactcheck.com.
Article return method based on recent publish date rather than popularity for effective breaking news observation.
